Egg Fried Rice

The egg fried rice is an ancient and delicious Chinese recipes. It is a fast and easy dish for busy people

Ingredients

  • Overnight rice
  • Raw eggs
  • Spring onions
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preparation: crack eggs into a big bowl; dice the spring onion; mix the rice and eggs together
  2. Heat the oil in wok and turn to medium heat. Then add the mixture from step 1. Cook until you don't see wet eggs.
  3. Add salt and spring onions. Turn to high heat and cook until rice is dry.
