How to cook Frozen Pork Dumpling

The frozen pork dumpling is a convenient Chinese dish. It will only take 10 to 20 minutes to allow you to cook this delicous dish.

Ingredients

  • You can buy frozen pork dumpling in supermarket

Steps

  1. Fill the pot at least half full and start with large heat until water boils
  2. Put the dumplings in and stir 10 seconds. Then prepare a bowl of cold water.
  3. When the water boils again, add the cold water and turn to medium heat.
  4. Add some salt to the pot. Allow the dumplings to cook for 10 minutes.
